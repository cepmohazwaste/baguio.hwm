Hazardous Waste Laws & Regulations

Key national laws and regulations directly governing hazardous waste management in the Philippines. These form the legal foundation for Baguio City's local program.

Primary Laws & Implementing Rules

  • Republic Act No. 6969 – Toxic Substances and Hazardous and Nuclear Wastes Control Act of 1990
    The primary law regulating the importation, manufacture, processing, handling, storage, transportation, sale, distribution, use, and disposal of toxic chemicals and hazardous wastes.
    Read full text on Official Gazette
  • Republic Act No. 9003 – Ecological Solid Waste Management Act of 2000
    Provides the framework for solid waste management, including requirements for separate handling of special/hazardous wastes (e.g., household hazardous wastes).
    Read full text on Official Gazette
  • DENR Administrative Order No. 2013-22 – Revised Procedures and Standards for the Management of Hazardous Wastes
    Detailed implementing rules for RA 6969, including classification, registration, transport, treatment, storage, and disposal of hazardous wastes.
    Download PDF from DENR-EMB
These national laws are supplemented by Baguio City's local ordinance (see Ordinance page) for localized implementation.
