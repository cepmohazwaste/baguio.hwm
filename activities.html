Upcoming Activities

Scheduled activities and initiatives by the CEPMO Hazardous Waste Management Section for the 1st Quarter of 2026.

1st Quarter 2026 Hazardous Waste Section Activities

The following activities are planned for January–March 2026:

  • Used Oil Collection in Baguio Central Business District (CBD)
    • Initial coverage: 12 barangays
    • Target: Food chain establishments and households
    • Storage capacity: 112 drums (200 liters each)
    • Establish collection points and integrate with regular solid waste collection schedules
    • Develop process flowcharts and collection guidelines
  • Coordination with Barangays and Haulers
    • January: Site visits to hazardous waste facilities
    • Schedule meetings with barangays
    • Schedule meetings with DENR-accredited haulers (Kerith, Aspuria)
    • Conduct market survey for required supplies (drums, XRF Spectrophotometer, spill kits/fire extinguishers, bins, etc.)
  • Inspections
    • Regular inspections of auto shops and junkshops
  • Motor and Cooking Oil Waste Management
    • Proper labeling of drums
    • Assessment of available and needed drums
  • E-Waste Collection and TSD Operation
    • Expansion and improvement of e-waste collection and treatment, storage, and disposal operations
  • IEC (Information, Education, and Communication Campaign)
    • Barangay-level awareness programs
    • Distribution of compliance posters
    • Training on proper handover and spill handling procedures
  • Inventory of 'For Disposal' Hazardous Wastes
    • Update and maintain comprehensive inventory
  • Eco Fair Participation
    • Planning and coordination for upcoming Eco Fair event

Specific dates and locations will be announced soon. Residents and businesses are encouraged to participate.

Stay updated! More detailed schedules and event invitations will be posted here and on CEPMO social media.
