About the Facility

The Hazardous Waste Temporary Storage Facility serves as the official consolidation and handling site for regulated waste streams generated in Baguio City. It operates under strict compliance with DENR-EMB standards, ensuring safe segregation, labeling, and storage.

Waste Handling & Tracking

The facility manages various hazardous categories including healthcare waste, batteries, lamps, chemical containers, and e-waste components. Documentation, manifest tracking, and accredited transporter coordination are implemented for full transparency.
